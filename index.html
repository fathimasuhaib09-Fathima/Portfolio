About Me

I am an undergraduate student specializing in UI/UX design, with a strong interest in creating intuitive, user-centered digital experiences. Through academic coursework and hands-on projects, I have developed a solid foundation in user research, requirement understanding, wireframing, prototyping, and applying UI design principles. I enjoy exploring how users interact with digital products and translating those insights into clear, functional, and visually consistent designs.


I have experience working on individual and team-based projects, where I collaborated with peers, analyzed user needs, and refined designs based on feedback. I value clarity, usability, and simplicity in design, and I am continuously improving my skills in usability testing, accessibility considerations, and responsive design. As a growing designer, I am motivated to learn, experiment, and apply best practices to create meaningful and engaging user experiences.
